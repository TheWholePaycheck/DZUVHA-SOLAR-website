Dzuvha Solar Manufacturing Plant

Our Business

Dzuvha Solar is a South African sustainable energy company with a Solar Photovoltaic Module(PV) manufacturing plant in Durban, Republic of South Africa. The proposed plant will have a capacity of 150 MWP (Megawatt Peak) output annually on one shift configuration, 300 MWP in a double shift configuration.

The Dzuvha Solar PV Module Manufacturing plant will be South Africa's third solar PV module manufacturing facility after ART Solar and Seraphin plants. This specific plant is a competitive relative to solar PV module imports to service the growing local and regional market.

The company has taken extensive measures with the help of it's promoters to ensure that the production of its products meet and exceed international regulatory quality standards.
